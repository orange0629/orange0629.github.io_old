Legend of Zelda (Remaster)

This project is finished with my groupmate Connor Young in 3 weeks.

Introduction

This is an Unity remaster version of the classic NES game "Legend of Zelda", along with a custom level design with new mechanisms. The custom level enables player to control two characters at the same time, overcome a series of puzzles, and finally defeat the boss.

Basic Control

  • Movement: WASD/Arrow Key
  • Main Weapon: X
  • Secondary Weapon: Z
  • Switch Seconday Weapon: Space
  • Switch Traditional/Custom level: 4
  • God mode: 1

  • Room Transition
  • Pushable blocks
  • Featured rooms (bow and oldman room)

    • My Contribution to Custom level

  • Double-pushable blocks
  • Buttons
  • Room Rotations
  • Several Puzzle Designs
  • Final Boss

    AntiVirus

    This project is finished with my groupmates in Unproductive Production (Connor Young, Kate Kim, Vincent Ma) in 4 weeks.

    Introduction

    Welcome, new employees, to Unproductive Computer Repair! You and a partner have been hired because you are some of the best tech support specialists in the business. Your mission: clean out every last bit of bugs and viruses from the computers of the masses! Use the combined power of your foam shooter and vacuum to overcome obstacles, fight enemies, and save the day!

    Antivirus is a two-player co-op Unity original game by Unproductive Production where the players take on the role of employees who work for a general-purpose IT company to take on different jobs for customers, literally traveling inside their computer to clean up viruses. You can utilize two key weapons, the foamer and the vacuum, to solve puzzles and kill enemies.

    Tourist Destination Introduction Generation Language Model based on Fine-tuned Flan-T5

    The project builds a generative language model which can automatically generate the introduction of tourist destinations based on the reviews from tourists. The model uses the sentence transformer and TextRank algorithm to find and pick up important sentences, and then used finetuned Flan-T5 model with appropriate prompts to encode and decode into an objective, highly generalized introduction.

    Tourist Destination Search Engine based on PyTerrier

    A smart search engine that can retrieve tourist destinations based on abstract queries using reviews and descriptions crawled on TripAdvisor. The search engine used Query Expansion, BM25, BERT, and feature-based Machine Learning Reranking to improve its performance on MAP and NDCG.

    The Criminal Incidents Data Analysis of New York

    A Data Analysis Report based on factors and patterns in New York City crime data (like crime time, place, type, criminal and victim characteristics). The project uses SparkSQL to deal with multiple large dataset, uses Pandas to do further analysis, and uses matplotlib, Seaborn, PlotNine to visualize the results, in order to find out the influential factors and patterns of crimes.

    Word Adventure Game based on ChatGPT API

    A simple application of ChatGPT API to develop a word adventure game. It is an open world game, but the world is supervised by ChatGPT with basic world settings. The world is full of NPCs of different identities and personalities, and players can either talk to them to get useful information or turn against them. Any behaviors of player will lead to different possibilities. Players are free to create their own story line in every play.

    Restaurant recommendation system based on cuisine similarity

    A simple graph based recommendation system based on the crawled restaurant data on TripAdvisor. Use BeautifulSoup to extract restaurant data from html, then use json to cache them, and finally use Flask to visualize recommendation results in website interface.
